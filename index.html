What Is the Chaotic Daily Oracle?

Your Questions, Answered

Everything you wanted to ask but were scared to

Are these oracle card readings accurate?

Think of the Chaotic Daily Oracle as your intuitive bestie who reflects back the energy you're already sitting in. The messages are designed to resonate with real situations and give you something to reflect on — not predict your future, but illuminate your present.

How often should I pull a card?

Pull as many as you need, bestie. Most people do one pull per situation per day for the clearest energy. Pulling multiple for the same thing can create confusion — trust your first card.

Is this different from tarot?

Yes! Tarot uses a structured 78-card system with deep symbolism and spreads. Oracle cards (like ours) are more free-form — less rules, more vibes. Great for quick guidance, daily check-ins, and people who are new to card reading.
